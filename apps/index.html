Die meisten Fitness-Apps sind zu voll, zu gamified oder zu unehrlich. Stand jetzt bauen wir aktiv, testen regelmäßig und passen laufend an. Most fitness apps are too bloated, too gamified or too dishonest. We are building something we would actually use every day ourselves — lean tools that genuinely fit calisthenics, boxing and a disciplined lifestyle. No dark patterns, no subscription bullshit. Right now we are actively building, testing regularly and adjusting continuously.
